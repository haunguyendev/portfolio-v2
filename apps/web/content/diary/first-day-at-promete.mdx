Today was my first day at Promete Technology. I walked in not knowing what to expect, but left feeling like I made the right choice.

## The Morning

Met the team — 4 developers, all friendly. The codebase is React + Node.js, which is exactly what I wanted to work with. My lead walked me through the architecture and I was surprised how clean it was.

## What I Felt

A mix of excitement and nervousness. The imposter syndrome whispered, but I reminded myself: I led a team of 10 at FPT Software. I can do this.

## Evening Reflection

Went to the gym after work. Cleared my head. Tomorrow I start on my first real task — a CRM feature. I'm ready.
