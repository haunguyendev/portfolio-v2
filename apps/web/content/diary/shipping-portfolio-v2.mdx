## It's Live!

After weeks of late-night coding sessions, design iterations, and endless Tailwind class tweaking — my portfolio is finally live. And it feels incredible.

This isn't just a website. It's proof that I can ship something from zero to production, end to end.

## The Journey

I started with a blank `create-next-app` and ended up with a fully responsive, animated portfolio with project showcases, an about page, and now a blog.

The hardest part wasn't the code — it was making design decisions. Every color, every spacing value, every animation duration felt like it mattered. Because it does.

## Lessons From Shipping

- **Done is better than perfect.** I could tweak gradients forever, but shipping is what counts.
- **Build in public.** Sharing progress on LinkedIn kept me accountable.
- **Tools matter less than execution.** Next.js, Tailwind, shadcn/ui — great stack, but the magic is in finishing.

## What's Next

Phase 2 adds the blog and diary system. Phase 3 will tackle dark mode and SEO. The roadmap is clear, and the momentum is real.

Today I celebrate. Tomorrow I build.
